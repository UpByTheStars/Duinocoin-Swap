How to use?

Simply tip our bot any supported tip.cc currency and follow the instructions given. It will exchange at a 0.95 rate, so you will loose 5% when you swap. This is because the bot need funding to stay alive and active

Security

Our bot is built in a way which makes sure that all your funds are safe. At no point does anybody have access to any of your wallets, and you can be confident that if you ever tip us the wrong currency or we don't have enough funds to process your transaction then we will tip it right back to you!
